Congratulations! You’ve completed your Accelerator with Mark. You know what most players don’t. You know how to turn bad games into great games, and great games into your best games. You’ve got the tools and the knowledge and can finally see where your career is heading.

But don’t let complacency spoil all your good work. Join the

Topform Inner Circle to get ongoing support from Mark and keep your mind focussed on consistent improvement.

The Inner Circle is a great way to keep your performance ‘topped up’ after you’ve completed the 60-day core program. Players often ask if they can continue to work with Mark, so having this support and resource available will allow you to develop even further and give you that long-term and sustainable impact on your game—and your life.

GOAL-ORIENTED SUPPORT

First, we’ll identify the key areas of your game where you want to continue to make progress, be that mentally or physically.

Through a daily or weekly questionnaire (you decide which suits you) we’ll monitor your progress and keep your improving.

And there will be monthly calls where we discuss how you’re doing, whether you’re having any difficulties and how we are going to make the next month even more productive. This will ensure consistent progression towards your game goals.
